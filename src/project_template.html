Robina Hospital

Qld Health

When

Jan 2010 July 2012

Where

Robina, Qld

Contract Value

$1,300,000

'Robina Hospital has doubled in size and is now the sixth largest in Queensland with 364 beds and a 24 hour emergency department, mental health and aged care services all in one location. KOBE worked intimately with the consultant GHD and Project Services to develop a full design and successfully delivered the staged opening of th eentire hospital over two and a half years.

'Working on this project whilst the hospital was still operating certainly presented its challenges, it was great to see the final result!'

Brian Kerins, KOBE Director

In 2009 KOBE was the successful tender from more than six establish companies in the Brisbane/Gold Coast region by providing a full turnkey solution and the most cost effective bid. The specification was developed by both GHD and Project Services and covered the full implemenation of new systems and expansion of existing systems to suit the hospital.

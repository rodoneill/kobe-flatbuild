About Us

Our Background

Established in 1999, KOBE is an independant security and automation company founded in response to the commercial requirement for our particular expertise in the ares of access control and closed circuit television.

Since this time KOBE has grown to provide companies and individuals throughout Australia and abroad with integrated services from electronic security, building automation and network design. With each field moving rapidly in its extent and complexity, KOBE has constantly placed an emphasis on training, development and research. This is why KOBE is now one of the leading authorities in the field of integrated technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide companies, individuals and householders with a centralised approach to current and emergent technologies in the integrated fields of electronic security and automation.
